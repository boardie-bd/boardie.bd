Brass: Birmingham

An Industrial Revolution Strategy Masterpiece • 2–4 Players • 60–120 min Playtime

Game Overview

Brass: Birmingham is an extraordinary economic strategy game set during the Industrial Revolution in England. Compete to build networks, develop industries, and dominate markets in one of the most immersive and rewarding strategy experiences ever created. Every decision matters — every coal mined, canal built, and brewery supplied brings you closer to becoming the **ultimate industrial tycoon**.

  • Elegant economic engine – balance debt, income, and resource flow to fuel your empire’s growth.

How to Play (in short)

• Build canals and railways to connect cities and industries.
• Develop technologies to upgrade your coal mines, iron works, and breweries.
• Sell goods to markets before your rivals to earn victory points and expand income.
• Balance debt and investments wisely — fortune favors the bold but punishes the reckless.
• Survive the transition from canals to rail and emerge as the most powerful industrialist.
