Pandemic

A Cooperative Strategy Board Game for 2-4 Players • Approx. 45 min Playtime

Game Overview

In Pandemic, *you and your team* assume the roles of elite disease-fighting specialists battling to save humanity from four deadly diseases that threaten the world. The clock is ticking, outbreaks are spreading, and only **coordinated teamwork** will let you win. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

How to Play (in short)

• Players take turns performing up to 4 actions: move, treat disease, share knowledge, build research stations.
• After actions, draw 2 Player cards: collect city cards to eventually cure diseases.
• Then draw Infection cards: disease spreads across the globe, and epidemic cards increase the danger.
• If too many outbreaks occur, or the player deck runs out, you lose — unless you’ve found cures in time. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
